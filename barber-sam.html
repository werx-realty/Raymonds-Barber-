In His Own Chair

About Sam

Sam Okafor built a following on beards. Not trims — beards: shaping a full one, rescuing a patchy one, mapping a neckline that follows your jaw instead of a straight line drawn across your throat.

Sam starts by finding your actual lines: the cheek line that suits your face, the neckline that sits where it should, the corners that make a beard look designed rather than grown. Then he cuts to those lines with shears and finishes the edges with a razor.

He is candid about growth patterns. If a shape won't work with what you've got, he'll show you the one that will, and give you a straight answer about how long it takes to fill in.

Every appointment ends with the maintenance conversation — oil, balm, comb, and how often to come back. He'd rather you look good in three weeks than just walking out the door.

“A beard is a haircut. It needs a shape, not just a length.”
