In His Own Chair

About Nick

Nick Alvarez came up through the shop the same way Marcus did — sweeping, watching, then cutting under Raymond's eye until Raymond said he was ready. Three years in, chair eight is his.

He handles the bread and butter of the shop: taper, crew cut, buzz with a fade, classic side part, neck clean-up. Straightforward cuts done properly, without the wait.

Nick is the easiest barber in the shop to get in with, which makes him the right call for a walk-in on a Saturday or a last-minute cut before an interview. He is fast without being rushed.

Raymond still checks his work at the end of the day. Nick says he'd be worried if he stopped.

“Raymond told me a clean taper is harder than anything flashy. He was right.”

Specialties

  • Classic tapers and crew cuts
  • Buzz cuts with a fade
  • Side parts and school cuts
  • Neck clean-ups and line-ups
  • Short-notice and walk-in appointments

Best Booked For
