In His Own Chair

About Devon

Devon Price learned the fade from Marcus and then went and found his own thing: freehand design. Bring him a reference and he'll cut it clean, or let him improvise and you'll leave with something nobody else in Bakersfield is wearing.

Burst fades, drop fades, hard parts, geometric patterns cut in freehand with a trimmer — this is the chair for the work that takes a steady hand and a good eye. Devon maps the design before the first pass and adjusts it to your head shape rather than forcing the reference photo.

He is also the shop's go-to for wave maintenance and brush cuts, and he'll tell you straight whether your hair pattern will hold a design or lose it in ten days.

Book him for the first cut of a new style. He's fast on the touch-ups after.

“Anybody can copy a photo. The trick is making it fit the head it's on.”

Specialties

  • Burst and drop fades
  • Freehand design and hard parts
  • Wave maintenance and brush cuts
  • Tapers with textured tops
  • Line-ups and edge work

Best Booked For

Design work, burst fades, and anyone who wants something a little different.
