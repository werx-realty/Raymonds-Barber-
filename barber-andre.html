About Andre

Andre Castillo came to Raymond's from a salon background, and he brought a scissor hand with him. Where most barbers reach for clippers, Andre reaches for shears — scissor-over-comb, point-cutting, dry-cutting to see how the hair actually falls.

That makes him the shop's specialist for the difficult heads: tight curls, coarse waves, fine thinning hair, double crowns and cowlicks that spring back the moment you leave. Andre reads growth patterns before he cuts a single hair.

He is also the barber to see about gray. Andre blends salt-and-pepper so it reads as intentional rather than accidental, and he's honest about when a cut alone will do the job — which is more often than people expect.

Expect a longer consultation and a slower cut. Expect it to still look right five weeks later, which is the whole point.

“Your hair already knows what it wants to do. My job is to agree with it.”

Specialties

  • Scissor-over-comb and dry cutting
  • Curly, coarse and textured hair
  • Fine and thinning hair strategies
  • Gray blending and natural finishes
  • Longer men's styles and grow-outs
