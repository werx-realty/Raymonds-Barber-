About Us

The shop Raymond built

Raymond Ellis opened this shop with a barber pole, a pair of clippers, and a rule he still keeps: nobody leaves the chair until it's right.

The pole is still turning, and the shop now runs eight chairs — each one manned by a licensed barber Raymond trained or hand-picked himself. We're the shop on Panama Lane where a farmer, a firefighter and a first-grader all wait on the same bench, and everybody gets the same attention.

No apps, no gimmicks. Just sharp clippers, hot lather, honest prices, and a conversation if you want one. Walk in, take a seat, and let us take care of you.
